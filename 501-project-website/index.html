About me

My name is YanYan Li, and I got my bachelor degree at University At Buffalo. Go Bulls! I am a first-generation international student, majoring in Applied Math with a minor in Statistics. I am proud to be graduating from UB this Spring 2022. I am also excited to further my education by pursuing a master degree in Georgetown University. I would also like to continue working under a professor to create amazing research so I can grow and develop professionally.


Other than continuing my study in the US, I enjoy being a food blogger in my social media. I find great value in showing my international friends the different types of foods in the USA. This helps me share my happiness with everyone. I hope by continuing my education in the US I can enjoy the lifestyle I am currently living in right now.
